Seminar procedure

The seminar will be held in accordance with the program in a mixed format as follows:

  • participants of the seminar, who are in Irkutsk, present their reports personally in the conference hall of the ESI SB RAS;
  • conference participants who did not come in person participate in the meetings and submit their reports online via the Zoom video conferencing service at the link;
  • • the seminar is held according to Irkutsk time (+5 hours to Moscow time);
  • time limit for presentations:
    plenary reports and lectures for young scientists - 1 hour (50 min. presentation + 10 min. discussion and questions),
    sectional reports - 20 min. (15 min presentation + 5 min discussion and questions).

Данные могут быть обновлены.
