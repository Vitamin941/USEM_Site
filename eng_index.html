The seminar was partially supported Russian science foundation's grant 22-21-00409.

The seminar is dedicated to the eightieth anniversary of Anatoly Solomonovich Apartsyn


Opening day (15.08.22) - a round table dedicated to the memory of Anatoly Solomonovich Apartsyn.


Closing day (19.08.22) - round table, summing up the results of the seminar.


The format of the seminar is mixed, working languages are Russian and English.
